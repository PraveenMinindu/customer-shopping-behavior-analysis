# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data from 3,900 purchases across multiple product categories.

The objective is to uncover purchasing patterns, customer segments, product preferences, and revenue drivers to support data-driven business decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

How can a retail company leverage customer shopping data to:

- Improve customer engagement
- Increase revenue
- Strengthen customer loyalty
- Optimize marketing strategies
- Identify high-performing products

---

## Technology Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- PostgreSQL
- SQL
- Power BI

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Preparation

4. Shipping Type Comparison
5. Subscriber Analysis
6. Discount Dependency Analysis
7. Customer Segmentation
8. Top Products per Category

### 4. Dashboard Development

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard presenting:

- Revenue Insights
- Customer Segmentation
- Product Performance
- Subscription Analysis
- Shipping Analysis

---

## Key Findings

### Revenue by Gender

Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.

### Subscription Impact

Subscribers demonstrated higher spending behavior and stronger customer loyalty.

### Shipping Preferences

Express shipping customers showed higher average purchase values.

### Customer Segmentation

Customer base was segmented into:

- New Customers
- Returning Customers
- Loyal Customers

### Product Performance

Several products consistently received higher review ratings and purchase volumes.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Boost Subscriptions

Promote subscription benefits to increase customer retention.

### Loyalty Programs

Reward repeat customers to encourage long-term engagement.

### Targeted Marketing

Focus campaigns on high-value customer segments.

### Product Positioning

Highlight top-rated products within marketing strategies.
